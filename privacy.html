Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Overview

HomeImprovCalc processes all calculator inputs (room dimensions, material quantities, cost figures) entirely within your browser. No data is transmitted to our servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect room measurements, project budgets, addresses, or any personal information you enter into our calculators.

Standard Analytics

We may collect anonymized page view data to understand which calculators are most useful and improve the site. This data is never sold or used for advertising targeting.

Cookies

Minimal first-party cookies may be used for unit preferences. No advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are used.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts for typography. No other third-party tracking services are used.

Changes

Policy changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
