ROI Reality Check

Remodeling Magazine's annual Cost vs. Value Report is the most widely cited source for home improvement ROI data. The highest-ROI projects are often the least glamorous: garage door replacements, new front doors, and attic insulation consistently return 80–100% of cost.

ROI varies significantly by region and price point. In a hot seller's market, almost any improvement adds value. In a soft market, buyers discount non-essential upgrades. Improvements that fix problems (roof, foundation, HVAC) tend to be more important to buyers than cosmetic upgrades.
