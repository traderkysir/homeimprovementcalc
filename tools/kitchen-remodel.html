Kitchen Remodel ROI

Kitchen remodels consistently deliver strong ROI at resale. A mid-range minor kitchen remodel returns approximately 80–85% of cost at sale. A major upscale remodel returns 50–60% — more spend, less return percentage.

The best bang-for-buck updates: new cabinet hardware, fresh paint, updated lighting fixtures, and new faucets. These cosmetic changes cost $2,000–$5,000 and can make a kitchen look significantly newer without a gut renovation.
