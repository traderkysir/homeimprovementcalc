Wallpaper Buying Tips

Always buy 10% extra to account for pattern matching, mistakes, and future repairs. Pattern repeat adds significant waste — a 24" repeat on a 9ft ceiling wall means you lose nearly 2 feet per strip.

Most wallpaper is sold in "double rolls" containing the same length as two single rolls. Check with your supplier whether the listed roll is single or double.
