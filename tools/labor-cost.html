Getting Accurate Quotes

Always get at least 3 quotes from licensed, insured contractors. Verify licenses with your state contractor licensing board. The lowest bid is not always the best — check references and reviews carefully.

For large projects, ask for a detailed written estimate that breaks down materials, labor, and timeline separately. Change orders should be agreed upon in writing before work begins to avoid cost surprises.
