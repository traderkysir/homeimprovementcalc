About HomeImprovCalc

Why We Built This

One of the most common (and expensive) mistakes in home improvement is buying the wrong amount of materials. Too little means a second trip to the store. Too much means wasted money sitting in your garage. HomeImprovCalc was built to eliminate both problems.

Our calculators handle the math for every major material category — paint, flooring, tile, concrete, lumber, drywall, roofing, and more — plus cost estimators for major renovations.

How Accurate Are Our Estimates?

Material quantity calculators are based on standard industry formulas and coverage rates. We always recommend adding a 10–15% waste factor for cuts and errors, which most of our calculators include by default.

Cost estimates for kitchens, bathrooms, and other renovation projects are based on national averages and should be used as planning benchmarks only. Actual costs vary significantly by region, contractor, and material choices. Always get 3 quotes from licensed contractors before committing to a project budget.
